About Me

Hey there! I'm an artist with a passion for capturing the world through acrylics. I'm usually found with a brush in hand, letting the colors flow. Painting is my go-to for unwinding and just getting into a different headspace.

My journey with art has been a lifelong one, evolving from simple sketches to complex acrylic pieces. I love experimenting with light, shadow, and texture to bring my subjects to life. Whether it's the vibrant energy of Glastonbury, the quiet beauty of a still life, or the majestic presence of animals, each piece tells a story.
